## Edge Settings

*   **Enable Edge Detection (`_EdgeDetectEnabled`)**: Toggles the outline effect.
*   **Edge Thickness (`_EdgeThickness`)**: Controls the thickness of the detected outlines.
*   **Edge Color (`_EdgeColor`)**: Sets the color of the outlines.
