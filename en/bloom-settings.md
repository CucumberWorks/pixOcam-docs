## Bloom Settings

<dl>
  <dt><strong>Enable Bloom</strong></dt>
  <dd><code>_BloomEnabled</code>: Toggles the bloom effect.</dd>

  <dt><strong>Bloom Threshold</strong></dt>
  <dd><code>_BloomThreshold</code>: Pixels brighter than this value will contribute to the bloom effect.</dd>

  <dt><strong>Bloom Intensity</strong></dt>
  <dd><code>_BloomIntensity</code>: Controls the overall brightness of the bloom effect.</dd>

  <dt><strong>Bloom Radius</strong></dt>
  <dd><code>_BloomRadius</code>: Controls how far the bloom effect spreads from bright areas.</dd>

  <dt><strong>Bloom Softness</strong></dt>
  <dd><code>_BloomSoftness</code>: Controls the softness or sharpness of the bloom falloff.</dd>
</dl>
